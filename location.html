What's Nearby

Santiagos Resort puts you right in the heart of Cavite's most scenic destinations. All within easy reach during your stay.

🌋
🌋 ~30 min away
Taal Volcano & Lake

One of the world's most unique geological wonders — a volcano island within a lake, within a larger island. Take a boat across Taal Lake for breathtaking close-up views. One of the Philippines' most iconic landmarks, right in your backyard.

Tagaytay City Ridge

Famous for its cool climate (18–24°C), panoramic views of Taal Lake and Volcano, and vibrant dining scene. Walk along the ridge, dine at cliff-side restaurants, and enjoy Tagaytay's legendary bulalo and fresh strawberry taho.

Sky Ranch Tagaytay

A family-favorite amusement park perched above the Taal Ridge. Ride the iconic Sky Eye Ferris wheel for 360° views of the lake and volcano. Fun rides, games, and attractions for kids and adults alike — great for large groups.

Known as the "Coffee Capital of Cavite" — a charming highland town with local coffee farms, farm-to-table restaurants, fresh produce markets, and quaint cafes. Perfect for a relaxed morning stroll before or after your stay.

Travel Tips

Getting Here & Around

🚗

Best Way to Get Here

Driving is most convenient. Take SLEX or CAVITEX then STAR Tollway to Alfonso. Use Waze for exact directions. Free parking on premises for multiple vehicles.

🌡️

Weather & Climate

Alfonso sits at ~600m elevation — expect refreshing 18–24°C year-round. Perfect for pool days without the Metro Manila heat. Bring a light jacket for evenings.

🛒

Stocking Up

Groceries and wet market are 5 minutes away in Alfonso town. Larger supermarkets are 20 minutes away in Tagaytay City. Stock up before checking in.
