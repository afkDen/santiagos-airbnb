Explore the Area

What's Nearby

Santiagos Resort puts you right in the heart of Cavite's most scenic destinations — all within easy reach.

~30 min away
Taal Volcano & Lake
Taal Volcano & Lake

One of the world's most unique geological wonders — a volcano island within a lake, within a larger island. Take a boat across Taal Lake for breathtaking close-up views of the crater. A must-see Philippine landmark right in your backyard.

Nature Scenic View Day Trip Photography
~15–20 min away
Tagaytay City Ridge
Tagaytay City Ridge

Famous for its cool climate (18–24°C), panoramic views of Taal Lake and Volcano, and vibrant dining scene. Walk along the ridge, dine at cliff-side restaurants, and enjoy Tagaytay's legendary bulalo and fresh strawberry taho.

Dining Scenic View Shopping Cool Climate
~20 min away
Sky Ranch Tagaytay
Sky Ranch Tagaytay

A family-favorite amusement park perched above the Taal Ridge. Ride the iconic Sky Eye Ferris wheel for 360° views of the lake and volcano. Fun rides, games, and attractions for kids and adults alike — great for large groups.

Family Amusement Park Views All Ages
~5 min away
Alfonso Town Proper
Alfonso Town Proper

Known as the "Coffee Capital of Cavite" — a charming highland town with local coffee farms, farm-to-table restaurants, fresh produce markets, and quaint cafes. Perfect for a relaxed morning stroll during your stay.

Coffee Local Food Farm Visits Chill Vibes

Travel Tips

Getting Here & Around

Best Way to Get Here

Driving is most convenient. Take SLEX or CAVITEX then STAR Tollway to Alfonso. Use Waze for exact routing. Free parking on premises for multiple vehicles.

Weather & Climate

Alfonso sits at ~600m elevation — expect a refreshing 18–24°C year-round. Perfect for pool days without Metro Manila's heat. Bring a light jacket for cool evenings.

Stocking Up

Groceries and wet market are 5 minutes away in Alfonso town. Larger supermarkets (Robinsons, Walter Mart) are 20 minutes away in Tagaytay City.
