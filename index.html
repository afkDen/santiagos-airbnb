About the Resort

Where Every Group
Finds Its Story

Inspired by industrial chic container design, Santiagos Resort is a one-of-a-kind retreat in Alfonso, Tagaytay, Cavite.

Whether it's a barkada getaway, birthday blowout, family reunion, or corporate team-building — we have everything your group needs to play hard and relax harder, all under one roof.

Comfortably accommodates your whole crew with 2 VIP rooms, 9 double deck beds, a driver's room, and 8 bathrooms. Base rate (up to 20 guests) from ₱20,000/night; up to 40 at ₱1,000/extra head.

Industrial chic architecture unlike anything else in the Tagaytay-Alfonso area. Every corner is photogenic.

4 bedrooms, 20 beds, 8 bathrooms — your whole barkada sleeps comfortably. Base rate covers up to 20 guests.
