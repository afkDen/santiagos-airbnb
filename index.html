About the Resort

Where Every Group
Finds Its Story

Inspired by bold industrial chic container design, Santiagos Resort is a one-of-a-kind retreat tucked in the cool highlands of Alfonso — just minutes from Tagaytay City.

Whether it's a barkada getaway, birthday blowout, family reunion, or company team-building — we have everything your group needs to play hard and relax harder, all under one roof.

Comfortably sleeps your whole crew with 4 bedrooms, 20 beds, and 8 bathrooms. Base rate is for 30 guests, up to 40 at ₱1,000 per extra head.
