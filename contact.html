FAQ

Got Questions?

Everything you need to know before booking your group getaway at Santiagos Resort.

Still Have Questions? →
How many guests can Santiagos Resort accommodate?+
The resort accommodates up to 40 guests total. The base rate covers up to 30 guests. For 31–40 guests, an additional ₱1,000 per extra head applies. Please declare your full headcount when booking.
What are check-in and check-out times?+
Check-in is after 3:00 PM and checkout is before 12:00 PM (noon). Early check-in or late checkout may be arranged subject to availability — message us in advance to confirm.
Are all amenities included in the rate?+
Yes — pool, karaoke, billiards, arcade, gym, basketball court, bonfire area, full kitchen, TV, Wi-Fi, and free parking are all included in your nightly rate. The listing has 22+ amenities — no extra charges to use any of them.
Are pets allowed at the resort?+
No — Santiagos Resort is a pet-free property to ensure a comfortable experience for all guests. Thank you for your understanding.
Should I book through Airbnb or directly?+
Booking through Airbnb gives you payment protection and a formal booking confirmation. You can also send an inquiry here or on WhatsApp to ask questions or check availability before booking.
Is Santiagos good for corporate team buildings?+
Absolutely! The resort is ideal for team buildings, company outings, birthday parties, family reunions, and barkada trips. The variety of amenities — pool, sports, karaoke, games — ensures every person in your group stays engaged.
What is the cancellation policy?+
Cancellation terms are managed through the Airbnb platform. Please check the listing for the current policy before booking. For direct inquiries, contact us to discuss arrangements.
How far is Santiagos from Manila?+
Santiagos Resort is in Alfonso, Cavite — approximately 1.5–2 hours from Metro Manila via SLEX and STAR Tollway, and about 15–20 minutes from Tagaytay City. Free parking is available on premises for the whole group.
